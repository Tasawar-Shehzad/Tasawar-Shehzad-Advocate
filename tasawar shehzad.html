About Us

Tasawar Shehzad Advocate is a highly experienced legal professional serving the Malakwal area. As an Advocate of the High Court, he offers expert legal services in civil, criminal, family, and revenue cases, as well as court marriages. Additionally, he holds the positions of Notary Public and Nikah Registrar in Malakwal.

  • Criminal Cases
  • Family Cases

  • Free Legal Aid for Needy People

Professional History

Tasawar Shehzad Advocate has a distinguished history of serving the legal community in Malakwal. He served as the Secretary of the Bar Association Malakwal from 2007-2008 and as the Deputy President of the Bar Association Malakwal from 2018-2019.
